Safety Requirements Specification

📋 Project Information

🔧 Machine Limits (ISO 12100)

ISO 12100 – Step 1: Determination of machine limits Define intended use, physical limits, time aspects and other boundaries that form the basis for risk assessment.

⚠️ Risk Assessment (ISO 12100)

ISO 12100 – Steps 2 & 3: Hazard identification and risk estimation Identify hazard sources, hazardous situations and possible harm. Estimate risk level based on severity and probability. The PLr / SIL CL determined here feeds into each Safety Function.

🛡️ Safety Functions (Chapter 5)

Specification of Safety Functions Each safety function shall include: functional behaviour, trigger conditions, required PLr / SIL CL, response time, reset & restart requirements, fault reaction, diagnostic requirements, architecture, and interfaces.

🌡️ 4 Operating Conditions

Chapter 5 §4 – Operating Conditions Describe the environmental and operating conditions under which the safety-related control system must function correctly. Important for component selection and validation.

✅ 11 Validation Plan

Chapter 5 §11 – Validation Define how each safety requirement will be verified: by test, measurement, analysis, or review of documentation.
